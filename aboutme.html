More About Dominique

Dominique is a passionate and purposed professional and healthcare leader. Focused on servant leadership through personal development coaching, culture management and mindset management. With over 14 years in nursing, Dominique has always had a passion for serving others to reach whole-body health. With personal life experiences of how essential mindset management is, Dominique can help ensure your mindset is principled based, positioning you for a H.E.R._story! (healed, evolved & refined).

My belief is that you are purposeful. Allow me to help lay the new foundation, expose the cracks to position you for PURPOSE, seal them and ELEVATE!  

Dominiques’s attributes a large portion of her mindset management to indoor cycle!

Dominique began her cycling journey in 2016 by way of her sister in love who was already smitten by the flywheel. Dominique quickly learned that cycling was the missing link that allowed her to release, to be free, to destress, to escape .... all while establishing a healthy exercise regimen. Affectionately known as DSpinn by her indoor cycle community, her energy is infectious. Dominique is sure to give you an experience on the bike like never before... a ride to motivate your mental, having BIG fun all while pushing YOUR passion on the pedals!
Sometimes you need the bike, other times the bike needs you!
